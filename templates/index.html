About ICFEST

ICFEST-2025 is an international conference focusing on advancements in VLSI, IoT, Embedded Systems, Machine Learning, Cyber security, and Power Systems. It brings together researchers, industry experts, and academicians to exchange ideas and foster collaboration. The event highlights emerging trends in VLSI design, IoT's transformative role in smart ecosystems, efficient embedded systems intelligent automation with machine learning, and solutions to pressing cyber security challenges. It also explores innovations in sustainable power systems for a greener future.

About SIT, Mangaluru

Srinivas Institute of Technology (SIT) is a prominent engineering institution in Mangaluru, established in 2006 under the A. Shama Rao Foundation. It is AICTE-approved, affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), and A accredited by NAAC. The institute offers a variety of programs on a 15-acre campus located at Valachil, along NH66, approximately 10 km from Mangaluru city. With over 3,000 students, SIT provides 13 undergraduate programs, 5 postgraduate programs, and 6 research centers.

Venue

How To Get Here

The conference will be held at the Seminar Hall, Srinivas Institute of Technology, located in Merlapadavu, Valachil, Mangalore. Situated on a serene 15-acre campus, the venue is easily accessible via various modes of transport.

By Plane

The nearest airport is Mangalore International Airport (Bajpe), located approximately 30 km from the venue. Taxis and app-based cab services are available for direct transport to the college.

By Car

Valachil is located along NH66, approximately 10 km from Mangalore city. Ample parking space is available on campus.

By Bus

Frequent buses connect Valachil to major parts of Mangalore city. The college is located near the Valachil bus stop, a short walk from the venue.

By Train

Mangalore Central and Mangalore Junction (Kankanady) railway stations are the nearest train stations, located approximately 12 km from the venue. Cabs and auto-rickshaws are readily available outside the stations.
